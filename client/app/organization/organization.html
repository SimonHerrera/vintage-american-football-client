Vintage American Organization

Organize your own Vintage American Football game...

One of our goals is to spread the game of Vintage American Football. The only way to grow the game is to make sure that everyone is following the same rules and using the same equipment.

The purpose of this page is to share the rules and equipment suppliers that we use as well as educate on football in the early 1900's through the 1920's.

V.A.F. - General Rules

Field of Play - 80 by 48.33 yards

Length of Game - 4 - fifteen minute quarters, running clock (or less if agreed upon by both managers)

Rosters - 12-18 players

Lineups - 9 on both sides of the ball

Scoring Points- TD = 6, Drop Kick = 4, FG = 3, Safety = 2, Drop Goal Kick = 2, Place Goal Kick = 1

Yard Lines - Kickoff = 30, Touchback = 10, First Downs 20-40-20, Free Kick from Safety = 10, Extra Point = 20, Drop Kick 20+
