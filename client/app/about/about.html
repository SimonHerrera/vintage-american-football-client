About Vintage Football

About...

Vintage American Football was organized in February of 2015. The idea of playing a game using 1920's rules was brought up to Simon Herrera by Chris Zimmerman. Simon's Rock Island Independent's website gave Chris the idea of challenging Simon and his team to a game. The goal was to revive the 100 year old football rivalry between the cities of Moline and Rock Island in the Quad Cities.

The two joined forces with former Augustana football coach Jim Barnes and they pitched the idea of a game in Douglas Park to Rock Island Parks and Rec. With the blessing of the Parks and Rec. the two moved forward with plans for a game in late August
